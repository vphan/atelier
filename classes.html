ADMISSIONS PROCEDURES

The ASCR has an open enrollment policy. Because we work closely with each student on an individual basis, it is important to meet in person before study begins. For the prospective student with an art background, a member of the ASCR faculty will review a portfolio of his or her drawings and paintings, originals when possible and if necessary, digital images or slides. Beginning students without portfolios have the opportunity to see the school and observe classes in session. Working with the new ASCR student in this way, we can help them assess their needs and develop a program tailored to their artistic goals.

STUDENT ENROLLMENT AGREEMENT CONTRACT

All students are required to sign a tuition payment contract.

Maximum class enrollment size is limited to 15 students in each class. If classes are already filled, the enrollment deposit will be returned or, if desired, a deposit credit will be included with registration on to a waiting list.

CANCELLATION AND REFUND POLICIES

Should a student decide to withdraw before entering into instruction, the Atelier School of Classical Realism must be notified in writing seven (7) days of the date of the agreement, where upon all agreements made in advance will be refunded.

Should a student withdraw prior to the elapse of the seven (7) day period and before entering into instruction, 90% of the total cost of tuition will be refunded.

Should the student withdraw or be dismissed after instruction has begun, but before completion of the session, 85% of the tuition will be refunded if termination occurs during first week of instruction.

Should the student withdraw or be dismissed after instruction has begun, but later than one week and less than completion of a fourth week, 50% of the tuition will be refunded.

After the first four (4) weeks of instruction, no refunds will be made. All refunds will be sent within thirty (30) days of withdrawal.
