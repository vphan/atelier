Brian Lei

Brian Lei is a professional artist and illustrator living in San Francisco. As a graduate of CCA (California College of Arts), he is noted for the realism and storytelling of his oil paintings. Brian received first place for the John Steinbeck Grapes of Wrath contest held by Comcast in 2007. As a talented muralist, his creations are installed in both San Francisco and the East Bay. Brian has shown in the Meridian Gallery in San Francisco and also in the galleries of CCA. He is admired for his technique, his figure studies, composition, and ideas bringing masterfulness to his artwork.
